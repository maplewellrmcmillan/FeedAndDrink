Strawberry Pie

1 Quart Stawberries

3/4 Cup sugar

1/4 Cup Water

1 1/2 tablespoons corn starch

Baked Pie Shell

Wash and hull berries

Crush enough to measure 1 Cup into a sauce pan

Combine crushed berries with sugar, water, and cornstarch

Bring to boil.

Cook and stir for about 2 minutes until thickened and clear

Arrange whole berries in shell and pour glaze over berries

Chill
