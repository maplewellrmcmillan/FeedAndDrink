Strawberry Freezer Jam

Ingredients

  • 1 Quart Strawberries
  • ¾ Cup sugar
  • ¼ Cup Water
  • 1 ½ tbsp corn starch
  • 1 Baked Pie Shell

Instructions

  1. Wash and hull the berries.
  2. Crush enough strawberries to measure 1 cup, then place them into a saucepan.
  3. Combine the crushed berries with the sugar, water, and cornstarch.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Cook and stir constantly for about 2 minutes until the mixture becomes thickened and clear.
  6. Arrange the remaining whole berries beautifully in the baked pie shell and pour the warm glaze evenly over the berries.
  7. Chill completely in the refrigerator before serving.
